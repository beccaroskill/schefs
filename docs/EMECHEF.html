Description

Are you ready to be enveloped by the smell of freshly baked kifli as you enter the room? To taste the tangy but sweet flavor of sour cherry soup? To be mesmerized by the caramel on top of a Dobos cake? If yes, then you have come to the right place. I make Hungarian foods you can order and pick up, but, if you feel like it, you are free to join me while I cook or just to eat your food with me. I mainly specialize in making pastries and desserts. You can select from things like kifli, pogácsa, Dobos cake, szilvás bukta (prune jam-filled pastries), and palacsinta. If you would like a meal, you have options like schnitznel with galuska, sour cherry soup, and rakott krumpli. Check out my Instagram @emesemarka for a more detailed list! I am pretty flexible with my offerings, so if you've heard of something Hungarian that's not on this list and you would like to try it, there is a good chance I can make it for you. I am also able to customize foods on the list. Please reach out!

About the Chef

Hey folks! My name is Emese and I'm a sophomore at Columbia College studying Biophysics and Math! I grew up in NYC, but my family is originally form Hungary. I love making all kinds of food--from baking bread to cakes to túrógombóc to tacos--and I find cooking/baking a good way to relax. If you come join me for a meal, I can tell you about some other things I love to do, like photography, scuba diving, traveling, and building boats out of trash!
