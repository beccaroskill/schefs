Description

Have you thought about having a Michelin-star experience in a Ruggles suite? Okay, we won’t have quite that, but making your own mayonnaise sauce or herb butter, and searing a salmon together might just be able to make up for it. Come, be prepared to work in the kitchen (or not, if you don’t feel like it). Fresh juice in one hand, you can wait for dinner to be served up! And then: À la table!
