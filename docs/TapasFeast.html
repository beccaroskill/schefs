Description

Local ingredients. Unique flavor combos. Plant-based eats. What could be better? I love family-style, try-this try-that kind of dining, because it’s the best of ALL worlds: you get to eat potatoes. You get to eat pasta. You get to eat veggies. You get to eat tiny little crostinis with all different kinds of toppings. And, after all that, there’s still a few dishes left for you to try. Why have one thing for dinner when you can have it all?

About the Chef

Hey! I’m Christa, I’m 18, and I’m a sophomore in CC studying Economics. I’m from Iowa, so I grew up cooking with ingredients I got on frequent visits to the farmers market (read: little shacks outside farms on the side of the highway). I’m not in Iowa anymore, and I’m definitely not interested in Iowa-style meat and potatoes dinners, but I still have the same passion for finding fresh, sustainably sourced ingredients.
