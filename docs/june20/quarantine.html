Description

This will be a space to share ideas, and get some inspiration for your next project or meal. Bring a snack (maybe from your garden if it's ready!) and come share a gardening project you have been working on. You can share a couple pictures of the growing/cooking process or give us a little tour of a current project. Projects don't need to be edible (they could be something like an aloe plant to treat those burns from sunbathing in the window.) All gardening projects, experience levels, and snacks are welcome!!!

What to Prepare

Be ready to share a bit about your project like: 1. How you got the idea 2. What you have planned for it (meal, business, just for fun) 3.What you would recommend to someone trying to create a similar project
