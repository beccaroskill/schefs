The End of the "Girlboss"

Tea • 7 spots available

Date

Saturday, July 25 at 6 PM EST

Description

In 2020, there has been a public reckoning with the concept of the "girlboss" and the often discriminatory environment she produced. Current and former employees of these brands and publications have come forward to talk about the racism, sexual harassment, and discriminatory practices so many of the bosses (most, if not all, white women) who were once lauded as feminists had replicated from their male counterparts. Crybaby Zine, an online and print zine run by young people, many who are in college, would like to discuss the public reckoning as well as the faux capitalist feminism that allowed for these companies to thrive.

What to Prepare

Not required, but definitely recommended reading includes: 1) Leigh Stein’s “The End of the Girlboss is Here” 2) Amanda Mull’s “The Girlboss Has Left the Building” 3) Amanda Hess’s NYT piece on the Wing 4) ...and the many other articles about Away, Refinery29, Man Repeller, and Thinx
