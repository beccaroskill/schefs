Climate Justice = Racial Justice

Breakfast • 7 spots available

Date

Wednesday, July 8 at 10:30am EST

Description

What is climate justice? Who is it for? How is racial justice related? Well, we're arguing that they're too intertwined to be separated. The people hurting the most because of the climate crisis are disproportionately BIPOC marginalized groups, and - no surprise here - the people causing the majority of climate change are wealthy white people. Come hang to ask questions with us, learn with us, all about how climate justice = racial justice.

What to Prepare

Please don't bring any hate! This is meant to be a reflective safe space to learn. I am a mixed queer latina and want to open up this space to anyone who wants to be here.
