Description

This Schefs experience is for everyone, especially those who are NOT planning on becoming educators or who know little about our country's education system. It is clear that American society is deeply broken. There is a way out. The problem is, the most important "way out" is not an immediate solution, but rather a long-term investment with returns that come far in the future. Our country's education system is a massive problem. Our country's education system is also the solution. We're going to talk about all things education, from its history in the US, to the contradictions in its philosophy, to its economic influences and potential, to its identity as the most important part of every individual's life. Let's chat, learn, and network to build a better future for everyone.

About the Host

Jane is so angry and so excited. She is angry because education is disregarded in our day-to-day lives. Education budgets are the first to get slashed when times get rough, teachers are horribly under-compensated for the work that they do, and schools have not only become pipelines to prisons but prisons themselves. She is so excited because people are finally waking up and seeing education as our way out of this shit-show of a present reality. She is so excited to meet other students passionate about education as a site of social justice work. She is so excited because we're going to move forward together. She is also so excited because she's going to try to convince you to trade your Goldman internship for a teaching certification. Just you wait.
