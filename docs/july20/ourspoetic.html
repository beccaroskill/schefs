Description

Our Poetica will be a time for anyone to read, hear, discuss and/or share a poem(s) of their liking. Whether it be their own work or the work of another poet, everyone should feel free to read a poem. Perhaps it’s one they particularly enjoyed or found profound or just couldn’t keep to themselves. And it goes without saying, all forms and types of poetry are welcomed! The act of sharing poetry has often left me with an awe for language, a respect for perspective, and a doubt on both the creative and physical limits of our lives. And so I hope to share that opportunity with others through this virtual meeting. And for those who rather just listen than read, more than welcome! My idea is that each person would share a poem followed by a quick group discussion and general affirmation of the work. Discussions will lead where they may. THIS IS NOT MEANT AS A WORKSHOP. Simply a space to freely exchange and enjoy the written form.
