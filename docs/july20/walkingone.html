Walking on Eggshells

Description

The world is a crazy place right now. The goal of this conversation is to put curious minds together to discuss the plethora of society shaking events that have occurred within the past few months. From the BLM movement to COVID; foreign policy regarding Hong Kong to the role of the Information Age/social media in it all. If you’re the type of person who likes to dig into worldly events objectively and with philosophical rigor - this SCHEFS session is for you! In a respectful way, I hope we can leave our biases at the door and dig deep into the problems we’re entering society as adults with. Come with diverse perspectives, anecdotes, and the love of learning & debate!

What to Prepare
