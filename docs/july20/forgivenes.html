Description

In Beyonce's 'Lemonade' she takes listeners through her experience reckoning with Jay-Z's cheating. She shares her feelings of suspicion, recognition, anger, depression, and ultimately forgiveness. Since then I've wondered what it means when we say we forgive someone whether that's a friend, partner, family member or just ourselves. In this experience we'll be discussing what forgiveness means, how we exercise it, who deserves it and whether forgiving really means forgetting. If nothing else let's talk about the masterpiece that is Lemonade.

What to Prepare
