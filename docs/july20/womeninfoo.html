We hope that people can connect over some badass women shifting the culinary scene right now! About the Host

Honor, Antigone, and Ruba were brought together by the Columbia Women's Rowing team, where they quickly learned that they would have no problem bonding over their shared love of food, fashion, and navigating the world as 6 feet tall, massive women. Now, they are all but inseparable (even during quarantine) and prove that three really doesn't have to be a crowd. They can roast, they can laugh, and they can eat. Hedonism is the name of the game, we're so glad you can join us to indulge as well/
