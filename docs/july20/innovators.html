Description

Join us with late-night snacks to talk about our future world and how the cities we redesign today will determine our planet's future. Whether it's a shift from federal to local governments having more decision making, to diverting investments towards 'green' solutions like vertical farms and renewable energy. Not to mention, educating our current and future leaders to handle the upcoming hardships from climate change and priming cities with physical and digital infrastructure to handle future influxes of immigration. Join our discussion on tactics to change the course of our future cities.
