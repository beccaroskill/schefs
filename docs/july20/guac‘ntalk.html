Guac ‘n Talk: Community Outreach in Quarantine

Guacamole snack • 7 spots available

Date

Sunday, July 19 at 4:00 PM EST

Description

I would like to host a conversation surrounding how to approach community outreach while in quarantine. A close friend and I back home in Yakima, WA, have been developing an organization that intends to provide meals, snacks, and critical hygiene supplies to those in need. What I have discovered through this project reaches far beyond realizing “how fortunate I am”; rather, the experience has further radicalized me and reinforced my education. I want to host this conversation in hopes of inspiring my peers to seek out community or mutual aid organizations in their communities or start their own. Generally speaking, I hope to discuss ways we can incorporate mechanisms of care and restorative justice into our interpersonal relationships.
