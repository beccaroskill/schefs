The Threesome You Don’t Want: You, Sex, and Societal Pressure

Drink • 7 spots available

Date

Description

Let's talk about sex, baby. Or, how society influences how we have sex. From bending over backwards to the collective fear of speaking up, we want to hear it all. Oftentimes, we feel pressured to act in certain ways or to like things in sex because of the media, peer pressure, and other external forces. We want to ~discuss~ this pressure! We want to know how you think this narrative affects you and how you think as a society we can change the narrative to create a world of comfort in one's sexuality.
