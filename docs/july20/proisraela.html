Description

I would like to hope that this conversation will cater to everyone, from those who are passionate and well-read about the Israeli-Palestinian conflict to those who simply want to learn more about the region. For one of the planet's longest-standing and most complex geopolitical issue, the Israeli-Palestinian conflict oftentimes struggles to find its footing in progressive dialog due to the strong emotional or spiritual connection each side associates with the land and people that have been directly affected. In this event, I would like to challenge my own preconceptions and learn more from each and every participant. Although I don't think we will solve peace in the Middle East in a 1-hour zoom call, let's do our part in normalizing productive dialog about this conflict on our campuses.

What to Prepare

There is NO required level of knowledge to participate in the call, but please feel free to bring sources and articles that you think can benefit the group!

About the Host

My name is Jonny Aizik, I am an incoming Junior in the Columbia-JTS joint program. As an Israeli-American immigrant, I have grown up learning about the Israeli-Palestinian conflict (with an Israeli bias). I think that this is a perfect time for people from both sides to step out of their echo chambers and move closer to a middle ground that can take into account the collective needs of all stakeholders.
