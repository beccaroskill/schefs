Description

Learn to make some of your favorite - yet pretty expensive - dishes from Dig Inn in your dorm or at home so you can eat them whenever you want for a lower price! This is a collaborative experience that aims to give you the skills to recreate the dishes at another time, and you can pick whichever of the items you would like to eat.

About the Chef

My name is Ellis Shapiro-Barnum. I am 18 years old and from Brooklyn, NY, studying Urban Studies and Sustainable Development at CC. I’ve been cooking since elementary school when I became very into watching cooking videos online and became inspired to cook meals for my family. I care a lot about urban farms and adapting agricultural practices for our world’s rapidly changing climate (still figuring out how to actually do that though!)
