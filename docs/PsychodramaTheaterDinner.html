Description

Drama, tears, a four-course modern American meal. This is no restaurant; it is psychodrama dinner theater. Join Dr Willoughby Huff and his patient Randall Girard as the two embark on a therapeutic journey that will titillate the senses and tug at the heart strings. Based on Dr. Huff’s experimental culinary therapy treatment, the desperate and unhinged Girard will have to boil, simmer, and bake his way through the past trauma of a race-car accident, ultimately integrating the fractured strands of his once-whole personality. But will Girard be able to do so before it’s too late? When you come to the psychodrama dinner theater, the only prognosis that’s promised is a thank-you from your taste buds and a doff of the hat from your imagination!
