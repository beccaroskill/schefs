Coffee Talk (and Coloring!)

Morning Beverage • 7 spots available

Date

Tuesday, May 19 at 10am EDT

Description

Join me (Devin Lewtan) in making non-dairy lattes (and coloring) and talking about new changes in our perspectives post-covid. We can discuss how to deal with uncertainty, how to practice mindfulness and being grateful, and can get a good caffeinated start to our day so that we can be productive in whatever it is we have found comfort in!

What to Prepare

You will need to have some way to make coffee, preferably have a blender, and a printer to be able to print out coloring sheets of your choosing from this link Https://www.lewtan.dev/coloring.html (I drew them!) You also need markers or colored pencils to color our sheets while sipping coffee and chatting.
