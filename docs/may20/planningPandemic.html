Planning During a Pandemic

Late Lunch • 4 spots available

Date

Monday, May 25 at 5pm EDT

Description

When New Year's Resolutions for 2020 came around, our current reality of a pandemic was unimaginable. The long list of goals and plans have all been shifted and changed. I hope to reflect on the many lost goals and plans, and the ways to fulfill them in different ways. Lets talk about this shift in our lives and future, and the flexibility needed to come to terms with these new plans.

What to Prepare

Bring your new year's resolutions for 2020.

About the Host

Kwolanne Felix is an ambitious student organizer, history lover, and Passion Planner enthusiast. As a sophomore at Columbia College like many others, she has been thrown into a whirlwind of uncertainty during this time as she still lives in NYC. She studies history and loves making new connections.
