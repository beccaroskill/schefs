Zest + Caustics=Good Morning Y’all

Breakfast • 4 spots available

Date

Description

What better way to start the day, than appreciating the beauty and complexity of the world we inhabit?
As humans we work and struggle to become perfect with the hope that it will make us happier. We often forget, that in being human true perfection is never achieved, it is only perceived. The natural world is perfected by design and can therefore be an excellent source of joy, when observed thoughtfully.
This experience will be centered around my two favorite modes of happiness (and vitamins) SUNSHINE + CITRUS FRUITS. My formula for living a life full of ZEST.
The experience will be hosted in the space underneath a prime example an ephemeral natural phenomenon that reveals complexity and beauty, rippling caustics. The caustics I will be showing are natural light formations caused by morning light reflected through hexagonal water molecule structures. Check it out in this video.
In our time together, I hope to converse about the little things in life you’ve observed, that make you happy, while enjoying a nutritious vitamin packed, zesty, breakfast.

What to Prepare

Find a natural structure that fits in your hand, that is fascinating to you. If you can eat it, even better.
