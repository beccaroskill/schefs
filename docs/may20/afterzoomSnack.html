Description

In the past new weeks, many of us have returned back to our childhood homes and felt an unnaturalness in suddenly settling a new self into old spaces. While this process can definitely feel stifling at times, I’ve also rediscovered a lot about myself that lived here many years ago. The spaces we’ve grown from are filled with relics, and there have been many small discoveries over the past month (postcards, old doodles, moth-eaten t-shirts) that have reminded me of lost or latent parts of myself. While we all munch on our favorite childhood after-school snacks (because the only thing cooler than Dylan and Cole Sprouse’s special edition Danimals squeeze was London Tipton’s talking mirror), I ask you all to find, engage with and bring a relic which reminds you that your past self was definitely hilarious, at least a little wise and maybe sorta badass.

What to Prepare

All guests need to sift through through their forgotten belongings to find one relic of their old school selves.
