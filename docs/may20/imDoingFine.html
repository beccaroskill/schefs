“I’m Doing Fine”: A Tea & Baked Goods Hour With an Emphasis on Self-Care

Tea • 4 spots available

Date

Sunday, May 24 at 4pm EDT

Description

I’m a graduating Barnard senior and this academic year has been emotionally exhausting. For all members of the Barnard/Columbia community. I know for me, this quarantine has helped me hone on my sub-par baking skills and has incentivized me to learn recipes from my grandparents that have further expanded my understanding that food, culture, discourse are inseparable. My overarching theme for this meal is comfort. In other words, I want to offer a space where we can all take an hour to eat foods that reminds us of the sweetness and comforts of home. Barnard was a home to me, and amidst all this chaos, I am reminded of the many gifts it gave me. That said, I want to encourage a laid-back dialogue/debrief of the year in terms of what brought us joy and what we loved about this year.

What to Prepare

Bring a cup of tea of preference. If possible, please bring a dessert that brings you comfort, that reminds you of home, or is just something you like.
