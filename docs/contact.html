College students. Knowledge exchange.

Schefs was started in February 2020 by Lola Lafia and Pedro Damasceno, both undergraduate students at Columbia University.

Schefs is a platform for college students to facilitate and participate in themed conversations over a meal on topics of their choice, using “mealtime” as a vehicle for community, dialogue, and knowledge exchange. We create micro-think tanks for students to share/learn from one another, physically and virtually, and to harness the collective intelligence of our generation.

Schefs wants to empower college students to not let their minds go idle during this time of social isolation, to initiate and participate in conversations about topics they care about, to create a kind of give and take platform using each student’s interests, skills, and knowledge base as the currency: you share with others, you learn from others.
